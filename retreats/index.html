Hosts

Samantha Piro

Samantha is a licensed professional counselor and a 500 hour RYT. She is passionate about combining the healing modalities of talk therapy and movement. Samantha loves to find the balance of stillness and play in nature.

Kelly Cvetovski

Kelly is a physical therapist and 500 hour RYT. She loves traveling and incorporating yoga into travels, whether that is taking classes around the state of Michigan or participating and teaching in international retreats.

Shelby Spahar

Shelby is studying hospitality management at Cornell University with plans to open up her own inn upon graduation. Shelby has been in the service industry for the last 10 years, traveling throughout the world, learning about food across cultures.
